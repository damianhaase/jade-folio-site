Samsung Support Globalization

Samsung UK, USA, and Globalization for All 64 Countries

As a lead UX designer, I helped the team to redesign the global portfolio of Samsung websites (across 64 countries) into one consistent and well organized design, by using a variety of UX based techniques and Digitas methodology.

My Contributions

  • Gap Analysis
  • Local Market Research
  • Wireframes (for Support, My Account, and Apps)
  • Module Index as duplicates and product centric tagging (see next page for details)

Specific Contribution: Support Content Restructuring

Samsung required help articles to be assigned to a specific product. Conventional wisdom was that the product-code-specific articles were most relevant.

Based on our sample research, I discovered that the subtype-level articles were the most relevant (E.g., 3D TVs, LCD TVs, Plasma TVs, french door refrigerators, etc)

Additionally, sampling showed most of these articles contained non- hierarchy and inheritance model for help content. Users donâ€™t always need to have their model number in front of them to search for a general solution.

Another advantage of this model was that a single help article can be indexed under multiple products, removing the duplicated articles.

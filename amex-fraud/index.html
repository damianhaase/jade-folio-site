American Express Fraud Verification

Composed revised flow and wireframe for American Express mobile site to satisfy new business requirements and technologies. Design was done mobile first, then desktop later.

Step 1. Alert & Call-back Number

If a potential fraud transaction is detected per card, an alert message is displayed upon user’s log-in. User needs to provide call-back number for agent to follow up. Call-back number form recalls user’s existing data (home, mobile, etc) or asks for alternative number, even foreign country code.

Step 2. Suspected Charge List

Suspected charges are displayed; user is required to select whether charge is a fraud or not. Iconography was recommended for better readability.

(Process Dropping State)

It was important to emphasize to users the importance of completing the process, but also to give freedom to drop the process.

Step 3. Charge Veri cation Details

All the exhaustive options were provided, from most likely to least likely.
